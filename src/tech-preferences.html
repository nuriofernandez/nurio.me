I love working with a variety of technologies. Here is a breakdown of my current preferences and what I enjoy using the most.

I love working with OVH. They provide excellent bare metal servers and infrastructure that I rely on. I use Kubernetes for container orchestration, but I have a strong preference for K3S by far due to its lightweight nature and simplicity.

My core languages are Golang, Javascript, PHP, and Java. Each has its place in my toolkit depending on the project requirements. But Go has a special place on my hearth, it's simplicity and powerful standard library makes me really happy.

For simple websites, I stick to Vanilla HTML, CSS, and JS. It's clean, fast, and efficient. However, for more complex applications, I prefer React for component driven design.

My desktop computer runs on Arch Linux/Gnome and my laptop is a MacBook. I enjoy the control Arch gives me and the polished experience of a portable but robust MacBook.
